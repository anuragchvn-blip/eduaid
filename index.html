Welcome to EduAid India

Your gateway to a better future. Connect with students and educators for mutual learning and support.

About Us

EduAid India is a non-profit initiative aimed at bridging the gap between students and educators. We provide a platform for students to post projects and seek help, while educators can offer their expertise.

How It Works

  1. Register as a student or helper on the EduAid India app.
  2. Post a project or offer help based on your skills.
  3. Connect with students and educators to provide support and guidance.
  4. Utilize the app's features to enhance your learning experience.

FAQ

How do I register?

Yes, you can post as many projects as you need help with.

Browse the available tutors and select the one that best suits your needs.
